Your American Dream Machines Await - Win Two V-Series Cadillacs

Our Cadillac Dream Giveaway is back with an all-new prize package in an all-new color. Are you ready to find two V-Series Cadillacs — the ultimate in American luxury and high-performance — parked in your driveway?

You have come to the right place.

All it takes is one lucky ticket for you to be the winner of the most powerful dream machines to ever wear the Cadillac nameplate.

Plus, if you are the winner, we will pay out $73,000 for the federal prize tax on your behalf.

Keep reading to learn more about these V-Series Cadillacs and how you can win both!

Prize #1: 2024 Cadillac CT5-V Blackwing

This color-matched grand-prize package starts with a Radiant Red Tintcoat 2024 Cadillac CT5-V Blackwing with a premium Jet Black leather upholstery. It delivers 668 horsepower from a supercharged 6.2L V-8 engine, hand-built in GM’s Corvette factory. It comes with all the bells and whistles you can imagine and a sticker price of over $109,000.

For 2024, all Cadillac CT5-V-Blackwings (including this one) celebrate the 20th Anniversary of the CTS-V with exclusive content.

This bad-boy Blackwing is loaded with over $7,800 of extra-cost factory options, like Driver Assist and Parking packages, an Ultra-View sunroof, Super Cruise, Performance and Data Video Recorder and red brake calipers.

We’ve added our signature Blackwing hood and side performance callouts and blacked out V-Series wheels.

Get ready to clear out your garage for this double dream come true because the second part of this grand-prize package is even more incredible.

Prize #2: 2024 Cadillac V-Series Escalade

If you’re the winner, you’ll also load your family, your friends and you into the all-American SUV the world wishes it could own – the 2024 Cadillac V-Series Escalade.

This is Detroit’s most powerful luxury SUV with 682 horsepower and 653 lb-ft of torque, thanks to a more powerful version of the Blackwing’s supercharged V-8.

The Escalade V features an exclusive leather seat interior for all three rows, leather wrapped interior trim and great factory extra-cost options like a Heavy-Duty Trailering package, 22-inch wheels, blacked-out emblems and nameplates and even a console refrigerator with freezer.

Road and Track summed it up when its editor said, “You’ll find yourself smiling after every press of the start button and every stomp of that accelerator pedal.”

Does it come with a premium price tag? Yes, over $162,000! But don’t let that stop you from making your wish come true. Make a donation today to help out veterans’ and children’s charities and this Cadillac Escalade V-Series could be yours.
