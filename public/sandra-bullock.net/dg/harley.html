Grand Prize #1 – 2024 Harley-Davidson Edition Ford F-250 4x4 Crew Cab Truck

The 2024 Harley-Davidson Edition Ford F-250 is what Harley fans have been waiting for ever since Ford redesigned its Superduty truck lineup. All new, the Harley-Davidson Edition Ford F-250 is loaded with so many premium features that pretty much everything comes standard.

This is diesel muscle at its finest, powered by a 6.7L High Output V8 Turbo Diesel engine pumping out 500 horsepower and 1,200 lb-ft or torque, making it the most powerful Ford Super Duty ever produced.

With over $40,000 of upgrades that transform this “Ford Tough” F-250 Lariat 4x4 Crew Cab into a Harley-Davidson edition, you’ll have the coolest Super Duty truck on the road when you enter and win.

Grand Prize #2 –2024 Harley Davidson CVO Road Glide ST Motorcycle

With your winning ticket, you’ll also receive a 2024 Harley-Davidson CVO Road Glide ST motorcycle, the most responsive and sophisticated performance bagger on the road. 

Think of Harley Davidson’s CVO division as you would Ford’s SVO, Dodge’s SRT and Chevrolet’s COPO skunkworks programs. For 25 years, Custom Vehicle Operation (CVO) models have represented the pinnacle of Harley-Davidson style and design. They are manufactured in limited numbers and draw upon the expertise and craftsmanship of the brand. Each CVO model is equipped with show-stopping finishes, advanced features, exclusive components, and attention to detail that borders on the obsessive. 

The 2024 Harley-Davidson CVO Road Glide ST is backed up with the street-scorching power of the Milwaukee-Eight 121 High Output powertrain. The V-twin engine, spec’d with a high-performance camshaft and intake, delivers 125 hp, 147 lb.-ft. of torque and an elevated 5,900 RPM redline. Plus, Harley-Davidson outfitted this top-of-the-line bagger with Screamin’ Eagle extreme-flow mufflers, performance suspension and a kick-a** audio system.

The 2024 Harley-Davidson CVO Road Glide ST performance bagger motorcycle represents Harley-Davidson’s pursuit of open road motorcycles bred for racking up miles in premium style and total control.

About our Partners:

Biketoberfest® celebrates a full-throttle lifestyle born on two wheels. Motorcycle enthusiasts come together during the four-day rally each year to enjoy beautiful Florida weather, live music, motorcycle racing at Daytona International Speedway, and miles of rides along famous A1A, historic Main Street or the scenic Loop. Come experience the Southeast’s best motorcycle rally featuring motorcycle shows, custom bike builds and hundreds of the industry’s top vendors throughout Daytona Beach. All roads lead to this rally. For more information visit Biketoberfest.org.
