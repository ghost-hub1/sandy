Win a Mustang Dark Horse and a Customized Bronco Raptor

Unbelievable, but true! The Ford Mustang is the only gas-powered muscle car still being produced in Detroit. Gone are the Dodge Hellcats! Gone are the Chevy Camaros! But the muscle-car game is far from over.

The proof? The brand-new 850-horsepower Ford Mustang Dark Horse in the Dark Horse and Bronco Dream Giveaway. Loaded with visceral excitement. Visual eye appeal. And a supercharged V-8 engine that rumbles like it is 1970 all over again.

And because good things come in twos, don’t fret about your garage getting lonely.

Here’s why. If you have the lucky entry, you’ll also find yourself the proud owner of a customized 2024 Ford Bronco Raptor built by CJ Pony Parts.

Taxes. Nobody likes them, right? We have those covered, too. We’ll hand over $45,000 in the federal prize tax to Uncle Sam.

Let’s get right to the grand prizes.

Win a Limited Edition 850-HP Ford Mustang Dark Horse

Meet the H850 Ford Mustang Dark Horse, an 850-horsepower, supercharged V-8 powered pony that claims the Mustang horsepower throne.

The 2024 H850 Dark Horse (named after its horsepower rating) boasts performance numbers that will bring a smile to your face - 0-60 mph in 3.2 seconds; quarter-mile e.t. of 10.9 second.

This grand-prize Mustang comes loaded with Vapor Blue Metallic paint, a black/blue interior, Recaro front seats, Premier Trim and the Dark Horse appearance and handling packages, a 10-speed automatic transmission, bringing its MSRP to $76,650.

And that’s just getting started. The H850 horsepower package adds an upgraded supercharger system, high-flow air induction, upgraded fuel injectors and fuel pump system, engine calibration and professional installation.

It is #6 in the limited edition series.

We think you’ll need a pretty cool daily driver, too, so keep on reading … and start making room in your garage. With a little luck, you’ll be the winner of two fast Fords!

To Go with it: A Customized Twin-Turbo 2024 Ford Bronco Raptor

If you have the winning ticket, we’ll also hand over the keys and the title to a customized 2024 Ford Bronco Raptor.

The Bronco Raptor comes with a twin-turbo V-6 engine from the factory, a Fox suspension system with greater off-road capability, and 37-inch all-terrain tires for maximum traction.

Plus, thanks to CJ Pony Parts, the leader in Mustang and Bronco parts, this built-to-the-hilt Bronco features enhanced-performance upgrades from some of the best names in the Bronco aftermarket-performance arena.

That means your new Bronco Raptor will come preinstalled with Ford Performance Power Pack and oil/air separator, DV8 bumpers and side steps, Eibach ProLift springs, an AWE Switchpath cat-back exhaust system with dual Bashguard tips, 17-inch Fuel Piston wheels, Oracle lighting and much, much more.

Two Fast Fords – Over 1,300 Horsepower. It Only Takes One Lucky Ticket to Win Both!

455-horsepower Bronco for on-road and off-road fun. 850-horsepower Mustang Dark Horse to bring muscle-car excitement into your life. Your donation today puts you in the running for this power-packed duo of Ford dream machines. Plus, we’ll pay $45,000 for the taxes.

So, what are you waiting for? Enter today for your chance to win!
