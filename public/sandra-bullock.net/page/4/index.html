Welcome!


Online since 2002, Adoring Sandra is dedicated to the Oscar and Golden Globe award winning actress Sandra Bullock. Sandra's varied and widely celebrated career has seen her take on roles in While You Were Sleeping, The Proposal, The Blind Side, Miss Congeniality and many more big screen successes. We're the largest (and oldest) fan site dedicated to Sandra and her career and our goal is to bring you the latest up-to-date information, photos and media on Sandra and her career. Please bookmark us and return for a regular Sandy fix.
